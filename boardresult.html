Maharashtra State Board Result 2025 for Class 10 & 12

Published: April 29, 2025

The Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education (MSBSHSE) is set to declare the much-awaited Class 10 (SSC) and Class 12 (HSC) results for the academic year 2024–25 very soon. Here’s everything students need to know.

📅 Expected Result Date

  • Class 10 (SSC): Expected in the 2nd week of May 2025
  • Class 12 (HSC): Likely in the 1st week of May 2025

The exact dates will be announced on the official MSBSHSE website and local news portals.

🌐 Where to Check the Result

Students can check their results on the following official websites:

  • mahresult.nic.in
  • mahahsscboard.in

📝 How to Check

  1. Visit the official website (e.g., mahresult.nic.in)
  2. Click on the link for SSC/HSC Result 2025
  3. Enter your roll number and mother’s first name
  4. Click “View Result”
  5. Your result will be displayed. Download or print it for future reference

📊 Grading System & Passing Criteria

  • Students must score at least 35% in each subject to pass
  • Grades are based on marks obtained, with distinctions and first/second class divisions
  • Re-evaluation and supplementary exam options are available

🔍 What After the Result?

For Class 10: Students can choose between Science, Commerce, Arts, or vocational streams based on their interests and marks.
For Class 12: Students can apply for undergraduate programs, professional courses (like NEET, JEE, CET), or jobs depending on their plans.

🎉 Final Words

The Maharashtra Board result is a major milestone in a student’s academic life. Stay calm, check your result carefully, and plan your next step wisely. Best of luck to all SSC and HSC students for their results!
